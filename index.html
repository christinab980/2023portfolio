About Me

I am a graphic and website designer based out of San Diego, California. After graduating with my Bachelors of Science in Business Marketing specializing in Integrated Marketing Communications, I got a job in the non-profit sector designing and creating content. Since opening my own business, Christina Barron Designs, I have gained more work experience the design field and now have enrolled in Digital Crafts to further my education in Web Development learning HTML, CSS, CSS Flexbox, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Node.js, React, Redux, and Sequelize. My favorite place in San Diego is Kate Sessions park looking over the ocean.
